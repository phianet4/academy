About Phianet Coding Academy

A coding academy built in Mubende Municipality, for the people of Mubende.

Our story

Phianet Coding Academy was founded to close a simple gap: quality, practical technology training that doesn't require leaving Mubende Municipality. Based near Kasana Playground, the academy gives students in and around Mubende a place to learn real, employable coding and digital skills without the cost and disruption of travelling to the city.

Our mission

To equip learners of all ages and backgrounds with practical coding, web development and computer skills — taught in a supportive, hands-on environment that builds both technical ability and confidence.

Our vision

A Mubende where young people and professionals alike have the digital skills to create jobs, build businesses, and compete in a technology-driven world — starting right here in their own community.

Our approach

Classes stay small so instructors can give real attention to every student. Lessons move from fundamentals to real projects quickly, because the best way to learn to code is to build something with it. Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to sharpen existing skills, our instructors meet you where you are.
